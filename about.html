ABOUT EA. AQUAYE FARMS CO LTD

E.A. Aquaye Farms Co Ltd is a Private Limited Company Registered under the Ghanaian Companies Act of 2002 to carry out agriculture-related business like Crop farming, Livestock Farming, Trading of agriculture products, and other agriculture-related businesses. The company started operation in 2013 up to 2017 when it was officially registered under Ghanaian Companies Act as an Enterprise and in 2019, it was converted to a Limited Liability Company. The Company is Located in the Central Region of Ghana and operates in other parts of the country.

Lead the way towards eradicating famine in Ghana and Export-oriented multiple horticulture, production, process, and marketing business as part of a profitable and sustainable group of businesses fulfilling its economic, social, and environmental mandate.

Core Values

Relationships:
Agriculture is a people business. The company strives to connect small farmers in the industry to share knowledge, resources, and grow together. Therefore, we encourage partnership and mentorship among the stakeholders along the value chain of our products.

Service:
The company focuses on serving individuals in the general community. The effort is through the creation of a reliable market with high-quality farm products that create satisfaction for both the consumers and small-scale farmers as well as equipping young farmers through the company's outgrower scheme.

Training & Development:
Agriculture is constantly evolving, and we want to meet the challenges of a changing world by promoting continual education and training to build on our talents and develop new skills in the best use of available resources among the members of the company's value chain.

Industry Advocacy:
The best way to help people understand the role of farmers and the life sciences is to share information. We encourage the youth and women to be advocates of our industry and ensure farmers (Majority youth) receive sustainable income from the best farming practices, which encourages more food production and reduces the unemployment ratio.

Shared Passion
We have all chosen our respective careers because we are excited and energized to be involved in agriculture and make a difference. This shared passion runs as a common thread that unites us with all other farmers in the industry.

Environment:
The ability to feed the future is vested in the proper conservation of the land and environment we are living in today. Biodiversity Conservation measures are part of our agriculture practices.
A. Aquaculture E.A. Aquaye Farms aquatic production is mainly of Catfish

B. Tree Crops 1. 250 Acres Palmnut and Coconut Plantation at Praso in Twifo Atimorkwa District (about 215 Km from Accra) 2. 100 Acres Rubber Tree Plantation In Goaso in Asunafo North Municipal District (about 452 Km from Accra)

C. Livestock E.A. Aquaye Farms owns 50 Acres Farm Land of which Poultry, Piggery, Sheep, and Goat make the main livestock project for the time being. The main products from our livestock range from: - Chicken Eggs - Goats and Sheep - Pigs

D. Formal, informal Training & Extension Services. E.A. Aquaye Company Limited facilitates links to both formal and informal training as well as extension services specifically to esteemed Out-grower scheme members, contracted farmers, young farmers, and women farmers. These farmers are treated as potential future suppliers of produce to the company's consumers.

E. Products Marketing E.A. Aquaye Company Limited invests in securing a reliable market for the products produced within the company as well as those products produced by outgrowers.

F. Out-growers Management. E.A. Aquaye Company Limited manages and equips a list of outgrowers who produce quality products to meet the company's standard demand.

G. Logistics and Products Transportation E.A. Aquaye Company Limited manages the transportation of farm produce to its customer/consumer destination points for processing and value addition.
